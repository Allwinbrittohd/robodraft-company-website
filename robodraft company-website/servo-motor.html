SGTH Series: Ball and Screw Linear Actuator

Servo motor driven solution for highly precise and fast-response linear motion.

Product Overview

TOYO’s **SGTH series** is an electric actuator featuring a servo motor. It is designed as a highly precise and efficient device used extensively for motion control in advanced automation systems.

The servo motor integrated into the SGTH series ensures **high-precision movement** with remarkably **fast response times** and the crucial ability to **hold positions precisely**. The servo motor operates with a sophisticated control system that continuously adjusts the motor’s position, speed, and torque based on **real-time feedback from the encoder**.

These electric actuators, combined with servo motors, provide a powerful, adaptable solution essential for demanding applications that require exact positioning and controlled motion across various industries, reinforcing their value in modern robotics and automation.

Key Features

  • **Standard Stroke:** Up to $\text{1250 mm}$.
  • **Extended Stroke:** Can be achieved by connecting rack gear, guideway, and aluminum extrusion.
  • **Movement Quality:** Ensures smooth and precise linear movement.
  • **Repeatability:** Guaranteed repeatability of $\pm 0.005\text{ mm}$.
  • **Performance:** High speed and acceleration up to $\text{1600 mm/s}$.
  • **Payload Capacity:** Maximum payload capacity of up to $\text{110 kg}$.
  • **Control:** Servo motor provides precise control over position, speed, and torque.
  • **Feedback:** Utilizes encoder feedback for continuous monitoring and correction.

Applications

  • Electric device assembly
  • Liquid filling
  • Welding
  • Press fitting of circuit boards and wafer boxes
  • Unit arrangement
