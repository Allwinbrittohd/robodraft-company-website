Toyo Motorless Ball Screw Actuators

Precision, High-Thrust, and Long-Stroke Actuation for Industrial Automation.

ETH Series – Compact Precision Actuator

High accuracy, compact linear motion solution with robust construction.

ETH Series Overview

The ETH Series actuator delivers compact, high-precision linear motion ideal for positioning, pick-and-place, carrying, and press-fitting applications. Its design focuses on maximizing performance while minimizing footprint. This series is engineered for efficiency and accuracy in tight spaces.

Key Features:
  • High accuracy and steady moment with secure reliability and quality.
  • Supports use with Stepper or Servo motors (Actuator with Motor).
  • Provides $\pm 0.01\text{ mm}$ of repeatability.
  • Supports payloads from $5\text{ kg}$ up to $150\text{ kg}$.
Typical Applications:
  • Electric device assembly
  • Liquid filling and welding
  • Press fitting of circuit boards and wafer boxes
  • Unit arrangement and precise small component handling

GTH Series – High Thrust Linear Actuator

Engineered for high-force applications requiring supreme accuracy and reliability.

GTH Series Overview

The GTH Series is a workhorse, designed for applications that demand exceptional thrust and precision. It features a robust recirculating ball screw mechanism for smooth, powerful, and efficient linear motion, even under heavy loads. Its heavy-duty construction ensures longevity in demanding industrial environments.

Key Features:
  • Standard stroke up to $1250\text{ mm}$ — extendable using rack gear, guideway, and aluminum extrusion.
  • Ensured repeatability of $\pm 0.005\text{ mm}$ for precise linear positioning.
  • High speed and acceleration capabilities up to $1600\text{ mm/s}$.
  • Supports maximum payloads of up to $110\text{ kg}$.
  • Outer layer treated with black oxide coating for corrosion resistance and durability.
Typical Applications:
  • Heavy-duty industrial automation
  • Robotics and cobots (large-scale)
  • Automotive steering systems (testing)
  • Wafer handling robots and SCARA mechanisms

GLTH Series – Long Stroke Linear Actuator

The economical choice for long travel, high-precision automation requirements.

GLTH Series Overview

The GLTH Series strikes a perfect balance between affordability, precision, and versatility. It is the go-to solution for applications in robotics, automation, CNC, aerospace, and medical fields where long-stroke movement is critical. This series provides extended travel capability without compromising on positioning accuracy.

Key Features:
  • Economical and durable design offering a balance of performance and affordability.
  • Supports stepper or servo motor integration for flexibility.
  • Compact and lightweight — suitable for limited-space installations.
  • Ensured repeatability of $\pm 0.005\text{ mm}$.
  • Stroke length up to $1500\text{ mm}$.
Typical Applications:
  • Circuit Board Surface Cleaning Device (long travel)
  • Coating Device for Small Applications (dispensing/rubberizing)
  • Cutting for Glass Substrate Boards (X-Y-Z systems)
  • Piling Device for Circuit Boards (receiver mechanisms)
