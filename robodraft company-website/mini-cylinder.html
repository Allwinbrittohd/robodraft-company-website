Toyo Mini Cylinders

Precision Electric Actuators for Micro-positioning and Miniature Applications.

Mini Cylinder - CSG Series

Actuator with Motor for high rigidity and extremely precise control.

Product Overview

Toyo’s Actuator with Motor **CSG series** is a high-rigidity mini cylinder designed for applications requiring **extremely precise and accurate control and placement** of large loads. It features a robust and dependable design and a high-quality **servo valve** that enables precise control over the cylinder's movement.

The utilization of a **ball screw driven mini cylinder** offers precise linear motion in compact spaces, making it a versatile solution across various industries. It is extensively utilized in sectors where accurate and dependable placement is essential, like manufacturing, aerospace, and automotive.

Key Features

  • **Small Form Factor:** Suitable for applications with **limited space constraints**.
  • **High Accuracy:** Ball screw mechanism ensures **accurate and repeatable linear motion**.
  • **Smooth Movement:** Ball screw design **minimizes friction and backlash**.
  • **High Load Support:** Capable of supporting **significant loads** despite its small size.
  • **High Rigidity:** Robust design for **challenging operating circumstances**.
  • **Precision Control:** Equipped with a high-quality **servo motor/valve**.

Applications

  • Micro-positioning systems
  • Miniature robotics
  • Optical and photonics
  • Medical devices
  • Automated Optical Inspection (AOI) systems
  • Micro-manipulation and Micro-assembly
  • Miniature actuated grippers and End effectors

Miniature Cylinder - CS Series

Compact, versatile ball screw driven linear actuators with enhanced straightness.

Product Overview

The **CS series miniature electric cylinder** is a compact and versatile linear actuator that provides precise linear motion. As a **ball screw driven** cylinder, it is commonly utilized in applications such as **robotics, automation, 3D printing, and medical devices**.

A key enhancement is the replacement of the profile linear guide with an **integrated linear bearing** to achieve an overall straightness of **±0.02 mm**. When selecting, factors like stroke length, load capacity, speed, and multiple control options (IO, Pulse, Communication) should be considered.

Key Features

  • **Compact Design:** Features a small size for easy integration.
  • **High Accuracy:** Overall straightness of **±0.02 mm** with integrated linear bearing.
  • **Low Noise:** Lower noise than comparable pneumatic cylinders.
  • **Easy Maintenance:** Designed for straightforward upkeep.
  • **Improved Strength:** Enhanced housing strength and smoothness.
  • **Multiple Control Options:** Supports **IO, Pulse, and Communication** methods.

Applications

  • Micro-positioning systems
  • Miniature robotics
  • Optical and photonics
  • Medical devices
  • Automated Optical Inspection (AOI) systems
  • Micro-manipulation and Micro-assembly
  • Miniature actuated grippers and End effectors

Micro Linear Actuator - CSGAZ Series

A combined solution of the CSG mini actuator with the Oriental Stepper Motor AZ series.

Product Overview

The **CSGAZ series** is an integrated solution combining the **TOYO CSG series mini actuator** with the **Oriental step motor AZ series**. The AZ motor offers compact size and high torque, and when mounted inline, it helps **reduce the overall application length**.

This solution is ideal for applications with **space constraints** and where only less force is required for pushing or pulling. Leveraging the **low-speed, high-torque characteristics** of the AZ motor, the micro electric cylinder achieves a maximum thrust of **460N** and a maximum stroke of **125mm**. Crucially, the need for a **gearbox is eliminated**.

Key Features

  • **High Thrust & Stroke:** Max thrust **460N**, Max stroke **125mm**.
  • **No Gearbox Needed:** Achieves performance without a gearbox.
  • **Compact Design:** Multiple motor lead directions and compact motor size.
  • **Easy Maintenance:** Can be maintained without opening the cover (easier greasing).
  • **Network Support:** Wide range of protocol support (**EtherNet/IP, EtherCAT, Modbus, CC-Link**, etc.).
  • **Absolute Encoder:** No batteries needed for the motor's Multi Loop Absolute Encoder.

Applications

  • Micro-positioning systems
  • Miniature robotics
  • Optical and photonics
  • Medical devices
  • Automated Optical Inspection (AOI) systems
  • Micro-manipulation and Micro-assembly
  • Miniature actuated grippers and End effectors
