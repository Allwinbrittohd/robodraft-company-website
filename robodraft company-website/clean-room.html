Toyo Cleanroom Actuators

High-Precision, Low-Particle Solutions for Critical Environments (ISO 14644-1 Compliant).

GCH Series – Built-in Linear Motion Guide Ball Screw Type

Precision, reliability, and performance tailored for stringent cleanroom requirements.

GCH Series Overview

The **Toyo GCH Series actuators** are known for their precision, reliability, and performance in various industrial applications, including semiconductor manufacturing. Cleanroom actuators must comply with ISO standards, particularly **ISO 14644-1**, which outlines the maximum permissible particle count in the air of a cleanroom environment. Actuators used in semiconductor cleanrooms are typically classified in accordance with ISO standards to ensure they meet the required cleanliness levels.

Toyo GCH Series actuators are engineered to provide smooth and accurate motion control, making them well-suited for tasks that require precise positioning and control, such as wafer handling, equipment positioning, and other critical processes within semiconductor cleanrooms.

Key Features:
  • **Low Particle Emission**: Cleanroom actuators are designed to minimize particle generation to prevent contamination of the cleanroom environment.
  • **Compliance with Cleanroom Standards**: These actuators adhere to specific cleanroom standards such as ISO 14644-1 to ensure that they meet the cleanliness and particle control requirements of the cleanroom environment.
  • **Smooth and Precise Motion Control**: Cleanroom actuators provide accurate and smooth motion control, which is crucial for applications that require precision positioning and movement.
  • **Sealed Design**: Cleanroom actuators feature sealed designs to prevent the ingress of contaminants and to maintain the cleanliness of the actuator itself.
Typical Applications:
  • Semi-conductor manufacturing
  • Wafer piling and handling
  • Wafer surface cleaning devices
  • Food processing industry (where sterile environments are required)

ECH Series – High Speed, High Payload Electric Actuator

Suitable for cleanroom applications with high demands on speed, payload, and stroke paths.

ECH Series Overview

In cleanroom environments, **electric actuators** play a crucial role in ensuring precision, quality, and cleanliness. We offer electric actuators that are suitable for cleanroom applications, with high demands on speed, payload, and stroke paths. Maximum payload of these electric actuators can be $150 \text{ Kg}$, and they have stroke paths of up to $1,500 \text{ mm}$. They are designed to minimize particle emissions and ingress of particles in cleanroom applications, adhering to stringent cleanliness requirements. Furthermore, the products are tested and classified in accordance with **ISO standards**, making them suitable for use in cleanrooms.

Key Features:
  • **Cleanroom compatibility**: These actuators are designed to minimize particle emissions and ingress, meeting the stringent cleanliness requirements of cleanroom environments.
  • **Precision and repeatability**: Electric actuators offer high precision and repeatability, ensuring accuracy in positioning and movement within the cleanroom.
  • **High speed and acceleration**: These actuators can achieve high speeds of up to $\text{1,600 mm/s}$, allowing for efficient and rapid movement within the cleanroom environment.
  • **Stroke paths**: Electric actuators can provide stroke paths of up to $\text{1,500 mm}$, accommodating a wide range of motion requirements in cleanroom applications.
  • **Compliance with standards**: The electric actuators are tested and classified in accordance with ISO standards, ensuring their suitability for use in cleanrooms and other controlled environments.
Typical Applications:
  • Semi-conductor manufacturing
  • Wafer piling and handling
  • Wafer surface cleaning device
  • Food processing industry
  • Optics and Photonics manufacturing
  • Medical Device Manufacturing
  • Pharmaceutical and Biotechnology applications

GLCH Series – Highly Sealed Cleanroom Ball Screw Actuator

Achieving high cleanliness and high performance through superior dust emission prevention.

GLCH Series Overview

The highly sealed structure of **GLCH series** achieves the dust emission prevention and improvement of the suction efficiency to establish both a high cleanliness degree and high performance. Industries like **semiconductor manufacturing** typically require a higher cleanliness level than medical device manufacturing due to the extremely dust-sensitive nature of delicate silicon wafers.

Many of today’s cleanroom actuators employ **ball screws**, while other designs use belt drives or rack-and-pinion arrangements. Actuators designed for cleanroom environments are typically connected to **vacuum systems** in order to remove any particles generated during motion. In the vast majority of cleanroom applications involving actuators, positioning repeatability to $\text{50 μm}$ is acceptable.

Key Features:
  • **Sealing design** can prevent dirt and foreign objects from penetrating inside.
  • Equipped with an **air fitting device**, which can keep the internal component clean.
  • Clean type can support **cleanroom grade class 10** (examined by SGS) widely applied in semi-conductor industry and food supply chain.
  • Effectively minimize the pollution of the actuator and working environment.
Typical Applications:
  • Semi-conductor manufacturing
  • Wafer piling and handling
  • Wafer surface cleaning device
  • Food processing industry

GLCB Series – Cleanroom Belt-Type Electric Actuators

Utilizes a belt-driven mechanism with a sealed design for controlled environments.

GLCB Series Overview

**Cleanroom belt-type electric actuators**, i.e., **GLCB series**, are specialized devices designed to be used in cleanroom environments where cleanliness and particle control are critical. These actuators utilize a belt-driven mechanism to convert electrical energy into linear motion, offering precise positioning and control while minimizing the risk of contamination.

GLCB series actuators typically feature an **enclosed design** to prevent contamination from external sources. Sealed enclosures help protect the internal components from dust, debris, and other contaminants, ensuring reliable operation in cleanroom settings.

Key Features:
  • Typically connected to **vacuum systems** in order to remove any particles generated during motion.
  • Provide specialized ports for quick connecting. This is for simple hookup to vacuum tubing.
  • Effectively minimize the pollution of the actuator and working environment.
  • Operation life is $\text{5000 km}$ when the product is using under specified conditions.
Typical Applications:
  • Semi-conductor manufacturing
  • Wafer piling and handling
  • Wafer surface cleaning device
  • Food processing industry
