About Us

Robo Draft Automation, founded in 2025, is a forward-thinking automation firm headquartered in **Chennai, India**. We specialize in **Control Panel Design, PLC Programming, Installation & Commissioning** for diverse sectors such as Automotive, Packaging, Pharmaceutical, Robotics, and General Industrial Automation.

Our multidisciplinary team combines engineering excellence with creative problem-solving to deliver precise, scalable, and high-performance solutions that empower modern industries. We take pride in developing automation systems that enhance efficiency, reduce downtime, and improve operational accuracy.

With a strong focus on **innovation, quality, and sustainability**, Robo Draft Automation continues to stay ahead of technological trends, ensuring every project reflects reliability and customer success.
