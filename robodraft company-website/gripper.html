Toyo Electric Grippers

High-precision, versatile electric grippers for modern automation and robotics applications.

Cam Type Gripper - CHG2 Series

Reliable and simple gripper mechanism utilizing Cams for robust material handling.

Product Overview

The Toyo **CHG2 cam type gripper** is a robotic gripper mechanism that utilizes internal **cams** to precisely grasp and manipulate objects. Cams are specially shaped mechanical components that convert rotary input motion into the linear movement required to open and close the gripper's jaws or fingers.

The **simple design and robust construction** of the CHG2 series make it known for its **reliability and longevity** in demanding industrial environments. This series offers an excellent balance of simplicity, reliability, and versatility, making it a popular and dependable choice for many core automation tasks.

Key Features

  • **Cam Mechanism:** Cams convert rotary motion into precise linear jaw motion.
  • **High Reliability:** Simple, robust design ensures excellent longevity.
  • **Versatile:** Offers a balance of simplicity and versatility for various objects.
  • **Robust Construction:** Built for harsh industrial environments and continuous operation.

Applications

  • Assembly line robotics
  • Material handling
  • Packaging systems
  • Pick and place applications

Worm Gear Gripper - CHZ Series

Specialized tooling for precision gripping, enabling smooth and accurate object manipulation.

Product Overview

The Toyo **CHZ worm gear gripper** is a specialized electric tool used in industrial automation and robotics for gripping objects with **exceptional precision**. This series integrates a **worm gear system**, which is essential for achieving smooth and highly accurate gripping, allowing for precise positioning of the gripper fingers.

CHZ grippers are designed for adaptability, allowing them to handle a **wide range of object shapes and sizes**, ensuring flexibility across various industrial applications. They are easily integrated into larger automation systems and communicate via standard interfaces like **digital IOs, fieldbus protocols (e.g., Profibus, Modbus), or Ethernet/IP**.

Key Features

  • **Worm Gear System:** Ensures **smooth and accurate** gripping movement.
  • **Precision Positioning:** Allows for exact and repeatable placement of fingers.
  • **High Adaptability:** Easily handles different object shapes and sizes.
  • **Durability:** Constructed with materials prioritizing long-term consistent performance.

Applications

  • Assembly line robotics
  • Material handling
  • Packaging
  • Pick and place applications

Linkage Gripper - CHY2 Series

Mechanical devices utilizing linkages for reliable, high-speed, and precise gripping.

Product Overview

**CHY2 series linkage grippers** are mechanical devices engineered for precise grasping and manipulation in various industrial settings. They operate using a system of mechanical linkages that efficiently convert input motion (usually from a motor) into the synchronized opening and closing of the gripping fingers.

Linkage grippers are highly favored in **automation, robotics, and material handling** due to their consistent **reliability and precision**. This series is specified to operate in highly dynamic environments, meeting technical standards for acceleration, frequency, and vibration control to ensure stable, high-speed operation.

Key Features & Specifications

  • **Linkage System:** Converts motion for synchronized, precise jaw action.
  • **High Reliability:** Consistent performance under demanding conditions.
  • **Max Acceleration Speed:** $300 \text{m/s}^2$.
  • **Action Time:** $3$ times for each $XYZ$ at $11\text{ms}$.
  • **Frequency Range:** $10 \sim 55\text{Hz}$ at $30\text{m/s}^2$ acceleration.
  • **Vibration:** Conducted vibration does not exceed $0.5\text{G}$.

Applications

  • Assembly line robotics
  • Material handling
  • Packaging
  • Pick and place applications
