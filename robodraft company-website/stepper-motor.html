Stepper Motor Actuators (Servo Cylinders)

High-precision linear actuators integrated with Closed-Loop Stepper Motors for dynamic control.

CGTH Series: Integrated Linear Bearings Servo Cylinder (Slider Type)

Converts rotary motion into precise linear motion with closed-loop positional feedback.

Product Overview

These actuators convert rotary motion into precise linear motion, thus earning the name "servo cylinder." They are designed to induce or control motion in mechanical devices. The **CGTH series** is a ball screw type actuator that comes with integrated linear bearings and is operated by a closed-loop stepper motor.

Closed-loop stepper motors utilize an encoder or other feedback device to constantly monitor the motor's position, allowing for precise control over rotational or linear speed and position. Toyo provides these actuators along with its motor and an optional controller, offering a complete, integrated solution. The feedback mechanism allows for real-time monitoring and adjustment of movement for specific application needs.

Features

  • **Position Control:** Accurate control over the actuator’s movement through precise feedback and positioning.
  • **Velocity Control:** Provides control over the speed of the cylinder's movement.
  • **Feedback Mechanism:** Sensors monitor the actuator’s position and velocity, allowing for real-time monitoring and adjustments.
  • **Double-Acting:** The actuators can move in both directions, allowing bi-directional motion.
  • **High-Precision:** Servo cylinders are designed for high-precision applications, making them suitable for industries such as robotics and aerospace.
  • **Standard Controller:** The standard controller provides PULSE, communication, and other control interfaces, and can be equipped with EtherCAT networks.

CGTY Series: Linear Actuator with Motor (Rod Type)

A rod-style servo cylinder combining high efficiency and precision for crucial positioning tasks.

Product Overview

The **CGTY Series** is a servo cylinder or servo actuator, typically a double-acting rod-type cylinder used in mechatronic systems. These actuators commonly include ball screws, which are highly regarded for their efficiency and precision, making them perfect for applications demanding accurate positioning.

The CGTY Series is integrated with linear bearings and comes as a complete solution with a closed-loop stepper motor. This integration simplifies installation and reduces overall system complexity. When coupled with our TC100 series controller, users can achieve enhanced performance, flexibility, and advanced automation capabilities in their systems, ensuring reliable and precise operation.

Features

  • **Improved Precision:** Enhanced accuracy in motion control by continuously monitoring and correcting the motor's position through an encoder-based feedback system.
  • **Increased Reliability:** The feedback mechanism detects and corrects errors, ensuring consistent and reliable performance.
  • **Energy Savings:** Closed-loop stepper motor actuators adjust power consumption based on actual load requirements.
  • **Standard Controller Support:** The standard TC100 controller provides PULSE, communication, and other two control interfaces, and can be equipped with EtherCAT networks.
  • **Low Maintenance:** Features low noise operation and easy maintenance.

CGCH Series: Integrated Cleanroom Servo Cylinder (Slider Type)

Ball screw driven actuator specifically designed for stringent cleanroom environments.

Product Overview

The **CGCH series** servo cylinder is a ball screw driven actuator that is specially designed for use in cleanroom applications. It includes integrated Linear Bearings for smooth and precise sliding motion. This system is provided as a combined solution with a stepper motor.

Movement control and programming are handled by the TC100 series controller. The controller features both Pulse control mode and EtherCAT for communication control mode, offering flexible integration into advanced manufacturing setups, particularly within the semiconductor and electronics industries where cleanroom standards are mandatory.

Features

  • **High Repeatability:** Provides exceptionally high repeatability of $\pm 0.005\text{ mm}$.
  • **Mechanism:** Ball screw driven with integrated linear bearings and a slider mechanism.
  • **Cleanroom Design:** Specifically designed and built for rigorous cleanroom applications.
  • **Payload Capacity:** Maximum horizontal payload capacity of up to $\text{30 kg}$.
  • **Stroke & Speed:** Features a maximum stroke of up to $\text{1000 mm}$ and a maximum speed of up to $\text{600 mm/s}$.

CGLTH Series: Ballscrew Actuator with Oriental Stepper Motor

A cost-effective co-branded solution for high-speed, precise ballscrew motion.

Product Overview

The **CGLTH series** is a result of a strong co-branding effort between TOYO and Oriental Motor, combining TOYO’s best mechanically designed ballscrew actuator with Oriental Motor’s high-performance stepper motor. This collaboration provides a cost-effective, low-profile, integrated-bearing motorized actuator solution.

Oriental Motor products are renowned for precision in the motion control industry. The CGLTH series leverages this expertise, making it integral for achieving automation efficiency in various applications, from driving conveyor systems to enhancing the precision of robotic arms.

Features & Applications

  • **Integrated Design:** Features a proprietary technology aluminum body with embedded rails and a specially integrated nut.
  • **Speed & Acceleration:** High speed and acceleration up to $\text{1600 mm/s}$.
  • **Moment Load Capacity:** Integrated rail design provides better moment load capacity.
  • **Longest Stroke:** Offers the longest ball screw stroke available at $\text{2500 mm}$.
  • **Encoder:** Includes a multi-loop absolute encoder; no batteries are required.

Applications:

Positioning, Pick and place, Carrying, Press fitting.

CGLTB Series: Belt Actuator with Oriental Stepper Motor

High-speed, long-stroke belt drive actuator using Oriental Motor's AZ hybrid control technology.

Product Overview

The **CGLTB series** combines a TOYO belt drive actuator with the high-performance Oriental $\text{AZ}$ motor. The $\text{AZ}$ series is particularly suited for long-stroke and high-speed positioning, which complements the belt-driven actuator perfectly.

The $\text{AZ}$ Step Motor utilizes an innovative hybrid control method, seamlessly blending the benefits of both open-loop and closed-loop control. It constantly monitors the motor's position, automatically switching control methods as needed. Furthermore, the belt drive motorized actuator does not require a gearbox, leading to high responsiveness and acceleration. With a sealed design and an exhaust connector, it is adaptable for cleanroom use in areas like semiconductor manufacturing.

Features & Applications

  • **Longest Stroke:** Provides a belt drive stroke of $\text{3000 mm}$ / $\text{50}$ pitch.
  • **Design:** No gearbox is needed, simplifying the mechanical design.
  • **Speed & Acceleration:** High speed and acceleration up to $\text{3000 mm/s}$ (Maximum acceleration is $\text{0.76G}$).
  • **Encoder:** Uses the $\text{AZ}$ motor, which comes with a multi-turn absolute encoder.
  • **Repeatability:** Provides a repeatability of $\pm 0.06\text{ mm}$.
  • **Safety:** After an emergency stop, the system can retrieve action from the exact stop position.
  • **Payload:** Can support a horizontal payload of up to $\text{15 kg}$.

Applications:

Positioning, Pick and place, Carrying, Press fitting.
