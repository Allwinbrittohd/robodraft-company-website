Toyo Motorless Rod Type Actuators

Precision Pushing, Pulling, and Vertical Positioning Solutions.

GTY Series – High Thrust Guided Rod Actuator

Robust solution for heavy loads, high thrust, and anti-rotation requirements.

GTY Series Overview

The GTY Series is Toyo’s heavy-duty rod type actuator, featuring a robust internal linear guide structure to handle significant pushing, pulling, and moment loads. It is specifically designed for applications requiring high thrust forces and precise, anti-rotation positioning. The built-in guide rods ensure stability and accuracy throughout the stroke, making it a reliable replacement for traditional pneumatic cylinders in challenging environments.

Key Features:
  • High thrust capability, up to $450\text{ N}$, for heavy-duty work.
  • Built-in linear guide rods provide excellent anti-rotation and high load moment capacity.
  • High repeatability of $\pm 0.02\text{ mm}$ for precise stops.
  • Max speed up to $1000\text{ mm/s}$, balancing speed and load.
  • Suitable for vertical applications requiring precise lifts and plunges.
Typical Applications:
  • Gate valve control and fluidic applications
  • Heavy material sorting and diverting in conveyor lines
  • Press-fitting and clamping operations
  • High-force inspection and probing systems
  • Vertical lifting mechanisms for heavy components

GTB Series – Compact Guided Rod Actuator

A space-saving solution with internal guides for stable push/pull motion.

GTB Series Overview

The GTB Series provides an excellent balance of load capacity and compact size. Designed with internal guides, it ensures smooth and stable linear movement, minimizing rod deflection under load. This series is often used in automation setups where space is constrained but reliable, repeatable motion is essential. It serves as an optimal electric alternative to pneumatic cylinders, offering full control over position, speed, and force.

Key Features:
  • Compact footprint with reliable built-in guidance for stability.
  • Handles static loads up to $50\text{ kg}$ depending on model size.
  • Max stroke capacity up to $1000\text{ mm}$.
  • High rigidity suitable for high duty cycle push-and-hold applications.
  • Provides consistent repeatability of $\pm 0.02\text{ mm}$.
Typical Applications:
  • Parts feeding and separation devices
  • Precision dispensing and gluing systems
  • Small-scale press-fitting and insertion tasks
  • Packaging machine pushers and ejectors
  • General purpose vertical lift and transfer units

Y-Series – Slim & Short Stroke Rod Actuator

Ultra-compact, non-guided rod type for light assembly and minute positioning.

Y-Series Overview

The Y-Series is designed for the most demanding space requirements. This series is extremely slim, lightweight, and engineered for short, precise movements involving light loads. It's the ideal choice for small electronic assembly, optical positioning, and light-duty material handling where a minimal footprint is critical. While it is non-guided, its precision mechanics ensure excellent repeatability and accuracy for its size class.

Key Features:
  • Minimal width and height, occupying very little machine space.
  • Ideal for light loads and high-precision short stroke applications.
  • Stroke range typically from $10\text{ mm}$ to $200\text{ mm}$.
  • Maximum thrust around $100\text{ N}$ for small component handling.
  • Exceptional repeatability of $\pm 0.01\text{ mm}$.
Typical Applications:
  • Component positioning in electronic assembly
  • Lens/sensor focusing and minute adjustment
  • Testing and measurement jigs
  • Actuators for small tooling and fixtures
  • Light-duty pick-and-place operation
