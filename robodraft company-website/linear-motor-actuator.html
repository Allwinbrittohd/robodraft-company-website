LTF Series – Iron Core, High Thrust Actuator

Maximum thrust and cost-effectiveness for long stroke, heavy-payload movement.

LTF Series Overview

The **LTF Series** is an Iron Core Linear Motor Actuator that converts electrical energy directly into linear motion using strong magnetic assemblies. The iron core design ensures the **highest possible thrust** while remaining cost-effective, making it specially designed for **high payload** applications requiring long-distance travel.

Key Features:
  • **Highest Thrust & Cost-Effectiveness:** Achieved through the robust iron core design.
  • **Long Stroke:** Maximum stroke length up to $4700\text{ mm}$.
  • **Exceptional Performance:** Offers superior force, accuracy, and reliability using magnetism.
  • **Low Wear:** Minimal structural wear ensures sustained accuracy over a long time.
  • **High-Speed Handling:** Suitable for high-speed transportation of large-volume objects.
Typical Applications:
  • High-speed transportation and large material handling.
  • Precise assembly and positioning of IT equipment.
  • Spray printing devices for PCB boards.
  • Barcode scanning devices.

LNF Series – Long Stroke Direct Drive Actuator

Eliminates backlash and offers extended stroke length for high-density, reliable motion.

LNF Series Overview

The **LNF Series** utilizes powerful permanent magnet assemblies and a high-density coil design for efficient linear motion. Being a **direct drive** system, it eliminates mechanical driving elements (couplings, etc.), thereby removing backlash and accumulated errors common in traditional systems like ball screws, significantly enhancing accuracy and extending service life.

Key Features:
  • **Backlash Elimination:** Direct drive design removes errors from additional converter devices.
  • **Ultra-Long Stroke:** Stroke up to $8000\text{ mm}$ (customizable).
  • **Extended Service Life:** Approximately 2 times longer than traditional ball screw modules due to the lack of driving elements.
  • **Advanced Control:** Standard controller supports PULSE, communication, CAN Open, and EtherCAT networks.
  • **High Efficiency:** High-density coil design supports dual-axis synchronous drive.
Typical Applications:
  • Aligning devices for pick and place of IC boards.
  • High-precision transmission positioning of test equipment.
  • CCD inspection and vision inspection machines.
  • Long-distance high-speed gantry systems.

LGW Series – Ironless, Low Cogging Actuator

Ultra-smooth, low-vibration motion for precision applications like semiconductor and optics.

LGW Series Overview

The **LGW Series** employs an ironless, electromagnetic drive system, which guarantees **cogging-free operation** for ultra-smooth motion, even at low speeds. It is designed for small, low profile, and highly accurate applications where low vibration is critical. Subtypes (LGTW, LGNW, LGCW) offer flexibility, including the LGCW which is a cleanroom actuator reaching a **CLASS 100** cleanroom standard.

Key Features:
  • **Cogging-Free Motion:** Ironless design ensures ultra-smooth and low-vibration operation.
  • **High Dynamics:** Maximum acceleration up to $2500\text{ mm/s}^2$.
  • **Enhanced Load Capacity:** Bigger rail spacing increases moment load capacity and payload.
  • **Simplified Maintenance:** Patented single oil nozzle lubricates both rail and slider.
  • **Cooling Efficiency:** One-piece motor and carriage enhance cooling and motor performance.
Typical Applications:
  • Semiconductor manufacturing process automation.
  • Photonics and laser processing.
  • Precision assembly of IT and biomedical systems.
  • High-accuracy vision and inspection equipment.

LCF Series – Linear Motor Core Design Focus

Understanding the differences: Iron Core, Core Less, and Shaft Bar Actuator Technology.

LCF Series & Linear Motor Structure

The core design of a Linear Motor Actuator dictates its performance profile. The **LCF Series** focuses on the Iron Core type, which provides high thrust and efficiency. This section outlines the three main types of linear motor structures used to achieve precise and consistent linear motion in automation, robotics, and industrial machinery.

Types of Linear Motor Actuators:
  • **Iron Core:** Offers the **highest thrust** and is the most cost-effective. Uses a flat magnet track with a mover (coil) wound inside a steel sheet.
  • **Core Less:** Provides very **smooth, cogging-free motion**, ideal for scanning and vision applications. Uses a U-shaped magnet track, with the mover (coil) fixed with resin/epoxy.
  • **Shaft Bar:** A type of ironless design, offering specific performance advantages due to its compact packaging and superior heat dissipation. Uses a round bar magnet stack.
Core Applications:
  • Iron Core: High force, heavy load, and high-speed applications (LTF, **LCF**).
  • Core Less: Vision inspection, precise scanning, and delicate component handling (LGW).
  • Shaft Bar: Compact design requiring good thermal management.

LGF Series – Integrated Cleanroom Actuator

Achieving high speed and precision with integrated linear bearings in a cleanroom environment.

LGF Series Overview

The **LGF Series** is a specialized cleanroom actuator that features an integrated linear bearing system for minimal particulate generation. Precise control is achieved by manipulating the magnetic field strength and polarity to create repulsive or attractive forces, resulting in smooth and repeatable linear motion. A key focus of this series is its thermal management system, ensuring high efficiency even during long stroke, high-speed operations.

Key Features:
  • **Cleanroom Ready:** Designed for use in controlled, particle-free environments (Cleanroom specific class not specified, but the cleanroom feature is primary).
  • **Exceptional Repeatability:** Long stroke ensures high repeatability within $\pm 0.002\text{ mm}$.
  • **High Speed/Low Noise:** Maximum speed up to $3500\text{ mm/s}$ with reduced operating noise.
  • **Superior Heat Dissipation:** Self-developed mechanism and aluminum teeth design quickly dissipate heat, increasing motor efficiency.
  • **All-in-One Performance:** Achieves long stroke, high precision, high speed, and low noise simultaneously.
Typical Applications:
  • Precision positioning in high-cleanliness environments.
  • Semiconductor wafer handling and processing.
  • Optical manufacturing and alignment systems.
  • Any application requiring $\pm 2\text{ \mu m}$ repeatability over long distances.
