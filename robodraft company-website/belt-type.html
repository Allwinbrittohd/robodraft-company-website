Toyo Motorless Belt Type Actuators

High-Speed, Long-Stroke, and Cost-Effective Linear Motion Solutions.

MG Series – Europe Type Belt Actuator

Unique support roller design for reduced contamination and vertical applications.

MG Series Overview

The MG Series, known as the Europe Type Belt Actuator, utilizes a specific **support roller design**. This innovative approach significantly reduces particle generation compared to traditional belt-type actuators that use LM guides, especially from the steel strip cover and carriage. A key feature is the ability to adjust the belt tension without disassembling the entire unit. This series is also well-suited for **vertical applications** and is chosen for its ability to achieve **higher speeds** than many other actuator types.

Key Features:
  • High-speed and longer stroke capability, ideal for long-distance movement applications.
  • Support roller design minimizes particle generation (cleaner operation).
  • Allows easy belt tension adjustment without unit disassembly.
  • Generally more affordable and produces less noise compared to ball screw actuators.
  • Utilizes a flexible belt/timing belt driven by a pulley system for motion transmission.
Typical Applications:
  • High-speed conveyor systems
  • Tape cutting and dispensing devices
  • Parts shifting between CNC machines
  • Industrial printing equipment
  • Adhesive dispensing systems

GLTB Series – Economical Belt Actuator

A low-profile, cost-effective solution for precision linear movement.

GLTB Series Overview

The GLTB Series is Toyo’s economical choice, specifically designed for **low-profile applications**. It provides efficient, precise linear motion across a wide range of industrial and commercial needs. Its advantages include smooth operation, cost-effectiveness, and low maintenance requirements. This series is built for engineers looking for performance without compromising on budget or space.

Key Features:
  • Economical series for integration into low-profile systems.
  • Maximum stroke capacity up to $3000\text{ mm}$.
  • Compatible with both Stepper motors or Actuator with Motor configurations.
  • Compact structure design, leading to lower costs and shorter installation times.
  • Provides high repeatability of $\pm 0.04\text{ mm}$.
Typical Applications:
  • Conveyor lines (general purpose)
  • Tape cutting devices
  • Parts shifting operations in manufacturing environments
  • Printers and plotters (large format)
  • General adhesive dispensing and light handling

ETB Series – High Speed & Ultra-Long Stroke Actuator

Designed for extreme distances and high velocity linear movement.

ETB Series Overview

The ETB Series represents a high-performance linear motion system specifically engineered for **long-distance movement** and **high speeds**. This electric actuator uses a motor to drive a belt mechanism looped around pulleys. The motor's rotational motion is efficiently converted to linear motion for the attached load or carriage. It supports some of the longest strokes in the Toyo lineup, making it suitable for large-scale automation projects.

Key Features:
  • High speed and ultra-long stroke, suitable for large applications requiring long travel.
  • Maximum stroke support up to $5000\text{ mm}$.
  • Maximum speed can reach up to $5000\text{ mm/s}$.
  • High speed based on maximum Actuator with Motor's RPM ($3000$) or Stepper Motor's RPM ($300$).
  • Compatible with Stepper or Actuator with Motor setups.
  • Provides consistent repeatability of $\pm 0.04\text{ mm}$.
Typical Applications:
  • Very high-speed conveyor systems
  • Large-scale tape cutting devices
  • Parts shifting and transfer over long distances
  • Industrial printing and large-format plotting
  • Automated adhesive dispensing in production lines
